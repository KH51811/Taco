Ground Beef

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Yellow onion
  • Olive Onion
  • Tortillas/Tacos
  • Freshly shredded cheddar cheese
  • Freshly shredded Monterey jack cheese
  • Either Salsa or Pico
  • Guacalome

Steps:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add the ground beef and onions for 5 minutes and break it up with the back of a wooden spoon.
  2. Add all the taco seasoning and mix. Continue to cook the ground beef and break it down with the back of a wooden spoon until it's fully cooked and no pink remains.
  3. Once cooked, add 2 tablespoons of water and reduce to a simmer. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the taco meat with the tortillas, shredded cheese, salsa or pico, or things you suggest.
Reference: How to make Ground Beef Taco
