Waffle Tacos Recipe Fun Facts
  1. The sausage was spongy and tasteless
  2. The eggs are reminiscent of the powdered
    faux eggs mixes served at schools
  3. The “table syrup” condiment did nothing
    to help tie the entire thing together
  4. The only redeeming quality is the light
    and crisp waffle
  1. Fry a large, thin sausage patty until browned and thoroughly cooked through
  2. Make a round waffle and use a spatula handle
    to help curve the waffle and ease the
    rip-pressure on the natural seam of the
    waffle. If you want to replace the waffle
    with a pancake, you should since pancakes are easier to handle and you can control their size
  3. Make scrabled eggs with 2 eggs
  4. To make it like Taco Bell does, the sausage patty had to be large, thin and flexible. The thing with sausage is that it shrinks in the
    pan. Make your patty about 50% larger than
    you think you’ll need and you should be alright
  5. Place the sausage in the waffle, fold and top with scrambled eggs
  6. Drizzle with maple syrup and enjoy
