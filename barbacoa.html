Barbacoa Tacos

Ingredients

  • Shredded Barbacoa
  • Avocado
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Tortillas
  • Other Seasonings + Dips

Instructions

  1. Cook Beef Barbacoa
  2. Add bay leaves + seasonings
  3. Season with salt and pepper and allow it to rest
  4. Cook low and slow (8-9 hours) OR cook on high for 4-5 hours
  5. Shred it and place it back into the slow cooked
  6. Assemble taco using Barbacoa and any extra preferences (cheese, herbes, vegetables, etc.)
