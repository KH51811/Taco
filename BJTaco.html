Black-Jack Tacos

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 small whole wheat tortillas
  • ½ cup rinsed and drained black beans
  • 2 tablespoons cheese
  • 1 tablespoon salsa
  • 1 shredded lettuce or cabbage
  • 1 cilantro
  • ¼ cup fat-free plain Greek yogurt
  • ½ tsp cumin spice and cream
  • 1 tsp lime or lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Top each tortilla with black beans and cheese or put in the microwave for 1 minute on high or until the tortilla and beans are warm and the cheese is melted.
  2. Add salsa and spice or cream to the top of the taco.
  3. Add the shredded lettuce or cabbage and cliantro leaves as toppings on your tacos
  4. Whisk together Greek yogurt, cumin, and lime or lemon juice in a small bowl until its smooth and mixed. You can season with Salt and Pepper if you want.
  5. Serve with 1 cup raw sugar snap peas or 2 tablespoon of cumin cream
