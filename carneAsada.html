Carne Asada

Ingredients

  • Skirt Steak
  • Fresh Orange / Lime Juice
  • Japapeno
  • Cilantro
  • Carlic
  • Other Seasonings

Instructions

  1. Create marinade
  2. Let steak marinate for a few hours and an extra 30 minutes on the coutner
  3. Season with salt and pepper and allow it to rest
  4. Cook with an indoor grill / pan (cast-iron skillet works) for 5-7 minutes each side
  5. Rest steak for approximately 10 mintues
  6. Cut speak to perferred size
  7. Place on taco and add any additional preferences
